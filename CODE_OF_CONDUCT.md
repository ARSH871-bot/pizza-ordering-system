# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, background, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, level of experience, or religion.

## Standards

Positive behaviour includes using welcoming language, gracefully accepting feedback, and focusing on what is best for the community.

Unacceptable behaviour includes trolling, harassment, or publishing others private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported by contacting the project maintainer via GitHub. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.

Adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org) v2.1.
